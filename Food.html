Japan In A Nutshell

    The Most Popular Foods In Japan:

  • Sushi/sashimi - Sushi, despite popular belief, is not raw fish but rather vinegared rice that is mixed with other ingredients, which may or may not include raw fish. Sashimi is thinly sliced raw meat, usually fish, such as salmon or tuna, served without rice.

  • Ramen - One of the most popular options in Japan. It is a bowl wheat noodles served in a soy sauce or miso soup mixed with many kinds of ingredients. Most typical ingredients include slices of pork, green onion, seaweed and egg.

  • Tempura - A Japanese fried snack made mostly from seafood and vegetables but can also be made from fowl and fish. It is seasoned with a sauce made with soy sauce, ginger and sugar. Tempura is often served with Tetsuyu sauce, which it is a mix of consomme, sweet sake, soy sauce, ginger, radish and spices.

  • Kare raisu - Basically rice with curry and a variety of meats and vegetables. Meats often used are chicken, pork, beef and sometimes duck. The vegetables used are primarily onions, carrots and sweet potatos

  • Okonomiyaki - A Japanese pancake made with flour, yam and eggs. A number of toppings can be added, the most common being green onion, beef, shrimp, squid, vegetables, mochi and cheese.

  • Yakitori - A Japanese brochette - A mix of vegetables and meat cooked in a grill and dipped in teriaki sauce.
